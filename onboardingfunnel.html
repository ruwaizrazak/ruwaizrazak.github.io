Design Process

Discover

Research on multiple datasets related to onboarding funnel.

Ended up using Tableau tables and Qualitative reports to define problem statement.

Definining Problem Statement

Users are churning out at the early stage due to early unlock which was then followed by forced engagement to multiple features.

Designing A/B Tests

Multiple A/B tests were designed to validate the experiements.

  1. Whether giving an option to users to navigate as per their wish reduces the churn?
  2. Whether Spacing out the feature unlocks helps in reduced churn?
Final Ramp

Intially ramped both versions to a small percentage of players, out of which, 1st A/B test was then ramped to 100% due to significance improvement in New User Retention Rate
